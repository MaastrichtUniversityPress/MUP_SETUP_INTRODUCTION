# Welcome to the Manual for Publishing Using Teachbooks


_Your starting point for writing and publishing your book._

Your book repository has already been set up for you by the MUP team. 
This means you don't need to worry about any technical configuration — 
everything is ready to go. Your job is simply to open the repository 
and start writing.

This manual will guide you through that process step by step, explaining 
any technical terms along the way. No prior experience is required.

If you'd like to go further and become a more advanced Teachbooks user, 
that's covered at the end of the manual too.

## What This Manual Covers

This manual walks you through everything you need to edit and publish 
your book. Here's what to expect:

1. **Understanding your repository:** What has been set up for you 
   and how to find your way around it.
2. **Editing your content:** How to write and format pages using 
   Markdown, the simple text format Teachbooks uses.
3. **Organising your book:** How to add, remove, and reorder pages 
   using the table of contents file.
4. **Previewing your changes:** How to check what your edits look 
   like before publishing.
5. **Publishing:** How to submit your finished book through the 
   GitHub interface.
